Bake Normals from High to Low Poly in Image
###########################################

The operator *Bake Normals from High to Low Poly in Image* bakes source mesh objects to a target object.
It is a convenience operator that simplifies the workflow of baking details from high poly objects to a low poly object with a transfer entry.
It creates the necessary transfer entry with the selected mesh objects and change the bake type to Normal in the transfer list.

.. admonition:: Usage:
    
    - Select the source mesh objects (high poly objects) and select the target object (low poly object) last.
    - Execute the operator *Bake Normals from High to Low Poly in Image*
    - Configure the size of the target image
    - Click on OK
    - Execute the operator :ref:`RGBA Bake <operator_rgba_bake>`
